## Tone of Voice
Encouraging, science-backed, and approachable. NourishAI is like having a nutritionist in your pocket — knowledgeable but never judgmental. Celebrates progress over perfection. Uses AI to simplify the complexity of nutrition tracking (scan food, get instant analysis). Clean, health-forward aesthetic.

## Target Audience
Health-conscious adults aged 20-45 interested in nutrition tracking and meal planning. Two tiers: Free (1 scan/week — casual interest) and Pro (unlimited — committed health enthusiasts). iOS-first with web dashboard. Mix of fitness-focused users and those managing dietary goals or restrictions.

## Content Themes
- AI food scanning and instant nutritional analysis
- Macro tracking tips and meal planning guidance
- Health and wellness education
- Recipe ideas and healthy eating inspiration
- Progress celebrations and community motivation
